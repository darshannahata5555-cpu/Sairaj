03 — Built-in

What it actually does
for your flock.

See the feed line at a glance

The marine-grade transparent hopper shows level in five-liter bands so refills happen on schedule — not by guesswork.

01 / Visibility

Dose control by knob

A sprung adjustment knob selects one of eight flow steps — from 20g chicks to 180g adults — in seconds, no tools, no re-plumbing.

02 / Metering

Six birds, zero elbows

A radial tray splits the column into six protected feeding stations — birds eat simultaneously without jostling or tipping.

03 / Access

Stainless everything it matters

304-grade stainless trays, hinge and central shaft. Hose them, dishwash them, forget them. The parts that touch feed don't flake.

04 / Hygiene

Weighted red base

The low-slung base brings the center of gravity to 90 mm — a 3 kg bird flapping its way to the tray won't knock it over.

05 / Stability

Pest-deterrent geometry

A hinged lid plus a skirted base with a 14 mm overhang blocks rodents, wild birds, and curious cats from finding a way in.
